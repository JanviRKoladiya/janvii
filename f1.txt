• Sales Force Automation (SFA) is a tool or software that helps you automate lowest value and menial sales tasks. 
• Sales Force Automation, or SFA, is "a technique of using software to automate the 
business tasks of sales"
• Sales Force Automation software automates many of the administrative duties so 
sellers can spend less time clicking around a CRM system and more time working 
with customers.
• The best SFA systems use artificial intelligence (AI) and unified customer data to 
prompt sellers to take recommended next-best actions.
